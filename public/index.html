My name is Deblina Mukherjee. I’m a student at the University of Chicago majoring in Sociology and minoring in Computer Science and Statistics. I’m from New Jersey.

Currently, I am half the EIC of the Chicago Shady Dealer, a member of Occam's Razor, an engineer at The Maroon, and the founder of UChicago's Open Data Project. In the ‘20-21 academic year, I’ll be the Civic Engagement Chair on the IOP’s Student Advisory Board, and the Chair of UChicago Student Government's Technology Committee.
