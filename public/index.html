My name is Deblina Mukherjee. I graduated from the University of Chicago in June 2021, with a major in Sociology and minors in Computer Science and Statistics. I’m from New Jersey, and now live in San Francisco.

I'm a data scientist and software engineer, currently at facebook (where I also previously spent two summers as an intern). I've also worked at Twitter, The Washington Post , Media Cloud , Civic Hall, UChicago's IOP, AAPI Data, VestHQ, and the IPE Lab.

At school, I was a member of Occam's Razor, the founder of UChicago's Open Data Project, and, from 2019 to 2021, half the EIC of the Chicago Shady Dealer. In the ‘20-21 academic year, I was also the Civic Engagement Chair on the IOP’s Student Advisory Board, and the Chair of UChicago Student Government's Technology Committee.
