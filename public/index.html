My name is Deblina Mukherjee. I’m a student at the University of Chicago majoring in Sociology and minoring in Computer Science and Statistics. I’m from New Jersey.

This summer, I worked for facebook as a data scientist and for Civic Hall as a research intern. In the past, I’ve worked for UChicago’s IOP, AAPI Data, VestHQ, and the IPE Lab. I TA’d for Computing for the Social Sciences in Spring 2019.

Currently, I’m half the EIC of the Chicago Shady Dealer, a member of Occam’s Razor, a research assistant in the Knowledge Lab, and the founder of UChicago’s Open Data Project.
